Important: By creating an account and using the OTEX platform, you confirm that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and processing of your personal data as described herein.

1 Who We Are

OTEX ("we", "us", "our") is an online binary options trading platform operated by OTEX Options Ltd, registered in Tasmania, Australia. Our registered address is available upon written request.

We act as the data controller of any personal information you provide to us. This means we determine the purposes and means of processing your personal data.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at info@otexoption.com.

2 Data We Collect

We collect personal data in several categories depending on how you interact with our platform:

2.1 Account & Identity Data

  • Full name, username, email address, and password (stored in hashed form)
  • Date of birth, gender, nationality
  • Phone number and residential address
  • Government-issued identity documents (for KYC verification): passport, national ID, driver's license
  • Selfie photographs for identity verification

2.2 Financial Data

  • Deposit and withdrawal transaction records
  • Cryptocurrency wallet addresses provided for withdrawals
  • Bank account details where applicable
  • Transaction hashes and payment proof screenshots
  • Account balances (live and demo)

2.3 Trading Data

  • Trade records including asset, direction, stake, duration, entry and exit prices
  • Trade outcomes, profit/loss history, win rates
  • Timestamps of all trading activity

2.4 Technical & Usage Data

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Device type, operating system, and browser
  • Session timestamps and login history
  • Pages visited, features used, and click patterns on the platform
  • WebSocket connection data for real-time trading features

2.5 Communication Data

  • Support messages and correspondence
  • Referral codes and referred user relationships
  • WhatsApp communications with agents (agent-facilitated transactions)
We do not collect or store your full credit/debit card details. Payment card processing, where applicable, is handled by authorised third-party payment processors.

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

Referral programme Tracking referrals and calculating bonus commissions

4 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity — processing required to provide you with the trading services under our Terms of Service (account creation, trade execution, payments)
  • Legal obligation — processing required to comply with KYC/AML laws, financial regulations, and court orders
  • Legitimate interests — fraud detection, platform security, analytics and platform improvement, provided these do not override your rights
  • Consent — where you have explicitly opted in to marketing communications or non-essential cookies

You have the right to withdraw consent at any time where consent is the legal basis for processing. Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing that occurred before withdrawal.

5 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our platform and public website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help the platform function correctly and to analyse usage.

5.1 Types of cookies we use

Type Purpose Required?
Session cookies Keep you logged in during your browser session Yes
CSRF cookies Protect against cross-site request forgery attacks Yes
Analytics cookies Understand how users navigate the platform (anonymised) No
Preference cookies Remember your settings (e.g. account mode, language) No

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent the platform from functioning correctly.

6 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your data in the following limited circumstances:

6.1 Service providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the platform. These include:

  • Payment processors (e.g. Flutterwave) — to process bank transfer deposits and withdrawals
  • Cloud hosting providers — to store platform data securely
  • Email service providers — to send transactional emails (KYC notifications, trade alerts)
  • Customer support tools — to manage support conversations

All third-party providers are bound by data processing agreements and are only permitted to use your data as instructed by us.

6.2 Agents

When you choose to use our P2P agent deposit or withdrawal service, your username, account reference (CR code), and the transaction amount are shared with the selected agent to facilitate your transaction. Agents are verified by OTEX and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

6.3 Legal and regulatory disclosure

We may disclose your personal data to law enforcement, regulatory authorities, or courts where required by applicable law, a valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of OTEX, our users, or the public.

We will never disclose your data to third parties for their own marketing or advertising purposes without your explicit consent.

7 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, regulatory, and business requirements.

Data Type Retention Period
Account information Duration of account + 7 years after closure
KYC documents 5–7 years from verification date (AML requirements)
Transaction records 7 years from date of transaction
Trade history 5 years from date of trade
Support communications 3 years from last interaction
Login & session logs 12 months
Marketing preferences Until you withdraw consent

When data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymise it in accordance with our data destruction policy.

8 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data, subject to our legal retention obligations
  • Right to restriction — request that we limit processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at info@otexoption.com with the subject line "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days. We may require identity verification before processing your request.

Some rights are subject to conditions and exemptions. For example, we are unable to delete data we are legally required to retain for AML/KYC compliance purposes.

9 Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect it, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption on all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
  • Encrypted password storage — passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and never stored in plain text
  • Encryption at rest — sensitive files (KYC documents, selfies) are encrypted on our servers
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) — available to all users to add an extra layer of login security
  • Access controls — only authorised personnel with a legitimate need can access personal data
  • Regular security reviews — we conduct periodic security assessments of our systems

Despite these measures, no system can guarantee 100% security. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by law.

10 Children

Our platform is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18 years. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a person under 18 without verifiable parental consent, we will take immediate steps to delete that data and close the account. If you believe we may have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us at info@otexoption.com.

11 International Data Transfers

OTEX operates from Australia and serves users across Africa and globally. Your personal data may be transferred to, and stored on, servers located outside your country of residence. In particular, data may be processed in:

  • Australia (primary data hosting)
  • Countries where our third-party service providers operate

Where we transfer personal data outside of your jurisdiction, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions, to ensure your data receives an equivalent level of protection.

Our platform and website may contain links to third-party websites, applications, or services (such as payment processors or social media platforms). This Privacy Policy applies only to OTEX. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites.

We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through our platform before providing them with your personal information.

Your continued use of the OTEX platform after any update to this Policy constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms. If you do not agree with the updated Policy, you must stop using the platform and may request account closure.
