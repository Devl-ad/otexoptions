Getting Started
6 questions
What is OTEX and what does the platform offer?

OTEX is a binary options trading platform that allows you to speculate on the short-term price movement of OTC (Over-The-Counter) volatility indices. You predict whether the price of an asset will Rise or Fall, or whether it will end Over or Under a barrier, within a set duration.

The platform offers both a Demo account (with virtual funds for practice) and a Live account (real money trading). You can also manage deposits, withdrawals, view trade history, and refer friends to earn bonuses.

We recommend starting with the Demo account to get familiar with the platform before trading with real money.
How do I create an account?

Creating an account on OTEX is free and takes less than 2 minutes. Visit otexoption.com, click Sign Up, and fill in your name, email address, and a secure password. You will receive a verification email — click the link inside to activate your account.

Once verified, you can immediately access the Demo account. To access Live trading, you must complete KYC identity verification.

What is a Demo account and how does it work?

A Demo account is a practice environment that comes pre-loaded with $10,000 in virtual funds. It works exactly like the real platform — you can place trades on all available assets, see real-time price movements, and view your trade history — but no real money is involved.

If your demo balance runs low, you can reset it at any time from the Dashboard by clicking the Reset button on the Demo Balance card.

Profits made on the Demo account cannot be withdrawn. They are for practice purposes only.
What countries are supported?

OTEX is primarily available to traders across Africa, including Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Uganda, Tanzania, and many other countries. We accept deposits and withdrawals in USD via cryptocurrency and agent (P2P) methods that work across Africa.

Bank transfer support for local currencies is coming soon. If you are unsure whether your country is supported, contact our support team before depositing.

What is the minimum age to trade on OTEX?

You must be at least 18 years old to create an account and trade on OTEX. This is verified during the KYC identity verification process. Any account found to belong to a minor will be suspended and funds returned.

Is OTEX available as a mobile app?

OTEX is currently a web-based platform optimised for both desktop and mobile browsers. You can access the full platform from any smartphone browser — Chrome or Safari recommended. Simply visit otexoption.com and log in.

A dedicated mobile app is in development. Follow our announcements for the release date.

Trading
8 questions
What types of trades can I place on OTEX?

OTEX currently offers two main trade types:

  • Rise / Fall — Predict whether the asset price will be higher (Rise) or lower (Fall) than its entry price at the end of the contract duration.
  • Over / Under — Predict whether the asset price will be above (Over) or below (Under) a specific barrier price at contract expiry.

Accumulator contracts are also available on select assets and allow your stake to compound tick by tick as long as the price does not breach a knockout barrier.

What assets can I trade?

OTEX offers a range of OTC Volatility Indices — synthetic assets that simulate real market volatility. Available assets include:

  • OTV19 — Volatility 19 Index (low volatility, slower movement)
  • OTV22 — Volatility 22 Index
  • OTV59 — Volatility 59 Index (medium volatility)
  • OTV90 — Volatility 90 Index (high volatility)
  • OTV115 — Volatility 115 Index (very high volatility, fast movement)

These indices run 24/7, meaning you can trade at any time — weekdays, weekends, and public holidays.

What durations are available for trades?

Available durations depend on the trade type selected:

  • 1 tick — The trade resolves after one price tick
  • 5 ticks — Resolves after 5 price ticks
  • 10 ticks — Resolves after 10 price ticks
  • 1 minute — 60 seconds
  • 5 minutes — 300 seconds
  • 15 minutes — 900 seconds

Tick-based durations are extremely short (usually under 10 seconds each) and are ideal for fast-paced trading strategies.

What is the minimum and maximum stake per trade?

The minimum stake per trade is $1.00. The maximum stake depends on your account tier and the asset selected, but typically ranges up to $1,000 per trade for standard accounts.

Verified (KYC-approved) accounts may have higher staking limits unlocked. Contact support if you need a higher limit for a specific trade.

What is the payout percentage?

The standard payout on OTEX is 85–95% of your stake on winning trades. For example, if you stake $100 and win with an 85% payout, you receive $185 total ($100 stake + $85 profit).

The exact payout percentage varies by asset, trade type, and duration. It is always displayed clearly before you confirm a trade.

If you lose a trade, your entire stake is forfeited. There is no partial refund except in the case of a tie (entry price equals exit price), which results in a full refund of your stake.
What happens if my trade ends in a tie?

If the exit price is exactly equal to the entry price on a Rise/Fall trade, the result is a tie. In this case, your full stake is refunded to your account and the trade is marked as "Refunded". No profit is made and no loss is incurred.

Can I cancel a trade after placing it?

No — once a trade is confirmed and placed, it cannot be cancelled or modified. All trades are binding from the moment they are submitted. This is standard for binary options trading.

Always double-check your direction, stake, and duration before confirming a trade.

Are the OTC indices real market prices?

OTC (Over-The-Counter) volatility indices are synthetic instruments — they simulate price movements algorithmically rather than tracking real-world financial markets like stocks or forex. They operate 24/7, including weekends and holidays, and are not affected by real-world news events.

The price feeds are generated by a certified random number generator, ensuring fairness and unpredictability in all tick movements.

Because these are synthetic indices, no fundamental analysis (news, earnings reports, etc.) applies. Trading is purely based on price action and probability.
Deposits
7 questions
How do I deposit funds into my OTEX account?

OTEX currently supports two deposit methods:

  • Agent (P2P) — Choose a verified OTEX agent, contact them via WhatsApp, and arrange a transfer. The agent manually credits your account once payment is received.
  • Cryptocurrency — Send Bitcoin (BTC), USDT TRC20, or Ethereum (ETH) to the provided wallet address, then submit your transaction hash for verification.

Bank transfer support is coming soon. Go to Wallet → Deposit to get started.

How long does a crypto deposit take to reflect?

Crypto deposits typically take 2–4 hours to be confirmed and credited after the transaction receives sufficient blockchain confirmations. During peak periods or network congestion, this may take up to 12 hours.

To speed up the process, always submit your transaction hash (TxID) after sending. This allows our team to verify your deposit without waiting for automatic confirmation.

Always double-check the wallet address before sending. Funds sent to the wrong address or on the wrong network cannot be recovered.
What is the minimum deposit amount?

Minimum deposit amounts vary by method:

  • USDT TRC20 — $10 minimum
  • Bitcoin (BTC) — $20 minimum
  • Ethereum (ETH) — $30 minimum
  • Agent (P2P) — depends on the agent, usually $10–$50
How do agent deposits work?

Agent deposits are a peer-to-peer (P2P) method ideal for users in Africa who prefer cash or mobile money. Here's how it works:

  • Go to Wallet → Deposit → Agents
  • Choose an available agent from the list
  • Click Contact on WhatsApp to message them directly
  • Tell the agent your username or CR code and how much you want to deposit
  • Send payment to the agent via mobile money or cash
  • The agent credits your OTEX account once payment is confirmed
Always include your CR code when messaging an agent — it's the fastest way for them to find your account.
Which cryptocurrencies are accepted?

OTEX currently accepts three cryptocurrencies for deposits:

  • Bitcoin (BTC) — on the Bitcoin network
  • USDT TRC20 — Tether on the TRON network only. Do not send ERC20 USDT.
  • Ethereum (ETH) — on the Ethereum mainnet
Sending USDT on the wrong network (e.g. ERC20 instead of TRC20) will result in permanent loss of funds. Always verify the network before sending.
My deposit hasn't been credited. What should I do?

If your deposit hasn't appeared after 4 hours, follow these steps:

  • For crypto deposits — check your transaction on a blockchain explorer (e.g. blockchain.com for BTC) to confirm it has at least 3 confirmations
  • Make sure you submitted your transaction hash in the deposit form
  • Contact support via WhatsApp or email with your transaction hash, the amount sent, and a screenshot of the transaction

For agent deposits — contact the agent directly on WhatsApp first. If unresolved, escalate to OTEX support with proof of payment.

Is there a deposit fee?

OTEX does not charge a platform fee for deposits. However:

  • Crypto deposits — network/gas fees apply depending on the blockchain. These are charged by the network, not OTEX.
  • Agent deposits — agents charge a small fee (typically 1.0%–1.5%) for their service. The fee is displayed on the agent's card before you contact them.

How do I withdraw my funds?

Go to Wallet → Withdraw. Choose your preferred withdrawal method (Cryptocurrency or Agent), enter the required details, and submit your request. Our team reviews and processes withdrawals within 1–24 hours after approval.

You must be KYC verified before making any withdrawal. Unverified accounts cannot access the withdrawal feature.

What is the minimum withdrawal amount?
  • USDT TRC20 — $10 minimum
  • Bitcoin (BTC) — $20 minimum
  • Ethereum (ETH) — $30 minimum
  • Agent (P2P) — depends on the agent selected
How long does a withdrawal take?

Withdrawal requests are reviewed by our team and typically processed within 1–24 hours after submission. Once approved:

  • Crypto withdrawals — arrive in your wallet within 1–3 hours depending on network congestion
  • Agent withdrawals — the agent contacts you via WhatsApp and arranges payment (usually same-day)

Withdrawals submitted over weekends or public holidays may take slightly longer.

Is there a withdrawal fee?

A 1.5% network fee is deducted from crypto withdrawals to cover blockchain transaction costs. Agent withdrawals are subject to the agent's fee (1.0%–1.5%) displayed before submission.

The exact amount you will receive after fees is always shown in the fee breakdown before you confirm.

What is the daily withdrawal limit?

Daily withdrawal limits depend on your KYC tier:

  • Tier 1 (Unverified) — Withdrawals not available
  • Tier 2 (Basic KYC) — Up to $2,000 per day
  • Tier 3 (Advanced KYC) — Up to $50,000 per day

To increase your withdrawal limit, complete or upgrade your KYC verification under Settings → KYC.

Can I withdraw to a different wallet than I deposited from?

Yes — you can withdraw to any valid cryptocurrency wallet address you own. There is no requirement to use the same wallet you deposited from. Simply paste your withdrawal address carefully in the withdrawal form.

Always verify the wallet address before confirming. Withdrawals to incorrect addresses are irreversible and OTEX cannot be held responsible.
Account & KYC
7 questions
What is KYC and why do I need to complete it?

KYC (Know Your Customer) is an identity verification process that confirms you are who you say you are. It is required by financial regulations and is used to prevent fraud, money laundering, and underage trading.

Without KYC verification, you can only use the Demo account. Completing KYC unlocks:

  • Live account trading with real money
  • Deposits up to $5,000/day
  • Withdrawals up to $2,000/day
What documents do I need for KYC?

You will need:

  • A valid, government-issued photo ID — Passport, National ID card, or Driver's License
  • A clear photo of the front (and back for ID card/license)
  • A selfie holding your ID next to your face

Documents must not be expired. All four corners must be visible in the photos. Scanned copies or screenshots are not accepted.

How long does KYC verification take?

KYC submissions are typically reviewed within 1–3 business days. You will receive an email notification once your application has been reviewed — approved or rejected.

During peak periods, review may take up to 5 business days. You can check your current status anytime under Settings → KYC.

My KYC was rejected. What do I do?

If your KYC was rejected, you will receive an email explaining the reason. Common reasons include:

  • Blurry or unclear document photos
  • Expired identity document
  • Name or date of birth mismatch
  • All four corners of the document not visible
  • Selfie face not clearly visible

Correct the identified issue and resubmit through Settings → KYC. Your previous information will be pre-filled so you only need to re-upload the corrected documents.

What is my CR code and what is it used for?

Your CR code (Client Reference) is a unique identifier for your OTEX account. It is displayed on your Account page and looks like: OTEX-JD-00042.

You should share your CR code with an agent whenever you make a deposit or withdrawal request via P2P. It allows the agent to instantly find and credit your correct account without needing your email or username.

Never share your account password — only your CR code. Copy it from Settings → Account with one click.
Can I change my email or username?

Username changes are not currently supported after account creation. If you need to change your email address, please contact our support team with proof of identity. Email changes are subject to a 48-hour review period for security.

How do I close my account?

Account closure is permanent and cannot be undone. Before closing, make sure to withdraw any remaining balance. You can request account closure from Settings → Account → Danger Zone.

Upon closure, all personal data, trade history, and account information will be permanently deleted in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Accounts with a pending withdrawal or open trade cannot be closed until those are resolved.
Security
5 questions
How does OTEX keep my account secure?

OTEX uses multiple layers of security to protect your account:

  • SSL/TLS encryption on all data in transit
  • Encrypted password storage — your password is never stored in plain text
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) — adds a second layer when logging in
  • KYC verification — prevents unauthorised account access
  • Session management — view and revoke active login sessions
What is Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) and how do I enable it?

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) adds an extra security step when logging in. After entering your password, you'll be asked for a time-sensitive code from an authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator or Authy).

To enable 2FA: go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication and click Enable. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app and enter the 6-digit code to confirm.

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA. It significantly reduces the risk of unauthorised access even if your password is compromised.
I think my account has been compromised. What should I do?

If you suspect unauthorised access to your account, act immediately:

  • Change your password immediately via Settings → Security → Password
  • Enable 2FA if not already active
  • Review active sessions under Settings → Security → Active Sessions and revoke any unfamiliar devices
  • Contact OTEX support immediately with details of the suspicious activity
OTEX staff will never ask for your password. If anyone contacts you claiming to be from OTEX and asks for your password, it is a scam.
Is my personal data safe with OTEX?

Yes. OTEX takes data privacy seriously. Your personal information and KYC documents are:

  • Encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Never sold to third parties
  • Only shared with regulatory authorities if legally required
  • Stored on secure, access-controlled servers

You can request deletion of your data at any time by closing your account or contacting our Data Protection team.

How do I recognise a phishing or scam attempt?

Scammers may try to impersonate OTEX. Be on the lookout for:

  • Emails from addresses other than @otexoption.com
  • Anyone asking for your password or 2FA code
  • Promises of guaranteed profits or "special investment opportunities"
  • Requests to deposit to an address not shown in your OTEX dashboard
  • Fake WhatsApp accounts claiming to be OTEX support

When in doubt, contact us directly through the official website or our verified WhatsApp support number.

Referrals

How does the OTEX referral programme work?

When you share your unique referral link and someone signs up through it, they become your referral. Every time that person makes a deposit, you automatically earn a 5% commission on the deposited amount, credited directly to your account.

There is no limit on how many people you can refer or how much you can earn. The more active traders you refer, the more you earn passively.

Where do I find my referral link?

Your referral link is available on the Referrals page. Navigate to Settings → Referrals and copy your unique link. You can also share it directly via WhatsApp using the built-in share button.

When is my referral bonus credited?

Your 5% referral bonus is credited to your account automatically as soon as your referral's deposit is confirmed. You can see a full breakdown of each referral's deposits and your earned bonuses on the Referrals page.

Can I withdraw my referral bonus?

Yes — referral bonuses are credited to your live account balance and can be withdrawn or used for trading just like any other funds. Standard withdrawal conditions and minimum amounts apply.
