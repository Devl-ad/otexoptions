FAQs
How long does verification take?
Most applications are reviewed within 1–1 business days. During peak periods it may take up to 5 days.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All documents are encrypted at rest and in transit. We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law.
What if my application is rejected?
You will receive an email explaining the reason. You can resubmit after correcting the issue — most rejections are due to blurry photos or mismatched information.
Can I use a passport from any country?
Yes, any valid international passport is accepted regardless of country of issue, as long as it is not expired.
